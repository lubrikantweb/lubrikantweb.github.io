Episode 5 - 'Wild Dreams' by Kolya

Casper Clark / Volte-Face · 💦LYLR 5💦 Wild Dreams by Kolya

WORDS FROM KOLYA “Dreams, dreams wild dreams!” This audiobook (!) starts off woozily and kinda boozily in some late-80s American locker room - hellooo high-school highballs! Soon the thick funk retreats and the heavy smog of minor chords loom - oh hello, interiority? Thank god that’ll never return (!?) Anyways, pretty soon we’re up in the sky riding some magic tea towel, with all kinds of ups and downs - whooosh! If cats are dreaming about their owners, and I certainly try to access my feline potential, I guess my dreaming self is owned by some sort of rollercoaster factory!! if you catch my gist?! - “Midnight fantasy!”

WORDS FROM CASPER CLARK Well now, this really is a special one! Not only have I never heard a single track on this mixtape before (always a good sign, and just the way we like it Chez Lubrikant), but it’s also put together by a dear friend whose singular ear has been apparent since our first encounters at the Lock Tavern some 20 odd years ago when he was but an eager chicken. It’s a diverse journey through obscurant corners of the electronicasphere, with a consistently exotic, woozy… dare I say it, dreamy vibe. You won’t hear anything else quite like it any time soon.
